FINANCIAL SERVICES

Accounting Outsourcing

Our suite of accounting services can replace entire accounting departments or fulfill more specific needs. We offer services to, and accounting management for, companies seeking temporary or start-up financial expertise covering corporate and treasury functions. Our clients gain immediate access to financial professionals to address any fiscal requirement.

SEC Compliance

Our expertise in, and state-of-the-art tools around, SEC requirements (e.g. Sarbanes-Oxley) will assist you in developing, establishing and documenting effective internal control structures and procedures. We perform risk assessments related to corporate governance processes, organizational transparency and internal controls to ensure that you employ best practices and are in compliance with SEC regulations.

Mergers & Acquisitions

We serve as advisors on all stages of business transactions. Services include performing due diligence, participating in negotiations, recommending optimal tax and accounting structures, analyzing alternatives and offers and advising on closing and transitions between parties.

Negotiations Preparation

Our experienced team of individuals will perform all the diligence necessary to certify that the client is making a well-informed decision on all types of financial negotiations. We provide clients a competitive advantage through insights into industry trends and best practices. We actively participate during the deal-making process and drafting of the agreement/contract.

Corporate Structuring

Our team develops domestic and international corporate financial structures with a focus on subsidiaries, acquisitions or recently merged companies. Our experts perform a meticulous analysis of current structures and procedures to develop recommendations and action plans that maximize your financial objectives. We support clients throughout the implementation of these changes.

Venture Capital

We have strategically partnered with venture capitalists and their affiliates to offer detailed and accurate accounting services. We provide management and financial officer guidance over their accounting and financial transactions relating to their investments. Sagent essentially acts as their “rental CFO” by becoming a valuable resource for investees with financial issues.

Treasury & Cash Management

We assist clients with their treasury functions by recommending investment and financing policies, providing compliance with accounting standards and reducing financial risk management. Other related services include the evaluation and improvement of cash management processes such as bank selection processes, bank relationship management (fee and account structure), cross border cash management structures, efficient tax structures, electronic banking and credit risk management.

TAX OUTSOURCING

Tax Savings & Planning

Our services offer clients a true partner in solving their tax issues. Through in-depth analysis and planning, clients minimize tax exposure that would otherwise negatively impact operations and business transactions. We guide clients on how to implement new tax methods and provide actionable recommendations and strategies that result in substantial tax savings.

Transfer Pricing

Our team of transfer pricing experts advises clients on how to plan for, manage, comply with, document and defend complex and dynamic issues around transfer pricing in order to ultimately maximize tax savings on a worldwide basis. With more countries challenging companies on their transfer pricing strategies and imposing onerous processes and penalties, our transfer pricing strategies aim to be commercially viable and balanced while generating tax efficiencies and mitigating the risk of challenges from fiscal authorities.

Multinational & Jurisdictional Tax Compliance

Tax compliance matters continue to proliferate at a rapid pace. We provide timely, expert advice and planning strategies on a range of tax issues such as income taxes, sales and use taxes, property taxes and VAT. As a result, our clients realize substantial tax savings while obtaining sustainable in-house expertise and improving administrative efficiency.

CONSULTING SERVICES

Business Operations

By collaborating closely with our clients’ management and staff, we recommend actionable strategies to improve their operations by implementing new technologies and procedures, eliminating redundant or unnecessary processes and increasing efficiency by filling gaps. We can also assist your business in matters relating to compliance with government regulations and laws, business license or tax issues and logistics around your staff and office space.

Human Resources Services

Sagent provides a holistic suite of HR services for clients, including payroll & taxes, benefits (healthcare, insurance, other voluntary benefits), retirement plans, workers’ compensation, risk mitigation, compliance and other customized HR services. Outsourcing your HR function to Sagent reduces your internal overhead and HR servicing costs and keeps your employees more productive while leveraging our always current domain expertise in HR.

Valuation Services

Our Valuation Services cover a multitude of areas including the valuation of equity positions, intellectual property, purchase price allocations and intangible assets. We also offer strategic advice on the structure and pricing of corporate transactions such as business sales, mergers and acquisitions and corporate restructurings. Further, we assist clients in the areas of financial planning and analysis, dynamic revenue modeling, strategic and market analysis, economic loss and damage calculations and project financing.

Management Oversight

Our Management Oversight services provide regular reviews and reports on the management and performance of subsidiaries, in order to provide parent companies with unfiltered insight. We specialize in gathering and analyzing large amounts of complex data where our unbiased, third party standing ensures the accuracy and integrity of reported results.

Leo Yen, President

Founder of Sagent Management, Leo Yen is the firm's President, responsible for directing the strategic and client operations of the company.
