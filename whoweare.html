Sagent Management is a global leader in Financial, Tax and Operational Services.

With over 100 professionals around the world – and growing – we have become trusted business partners to founders, investors, CEOs and CFOs of leading high-growth companies.

Our clients range from early-stage startups to Fortune 1000 companies that operate in high-tech, biotechnology, clean technology and other industries.

The Sagent team features professionals with vast global expertise and significant client-side experience. Each of our lines of service (e.g. CFO Services, Accounting, Tax, Human Resources, Business Regulations & Operations, etc.) are led by a Worldwide Practice Director.

Further, we have built a global ecosystem of investors, vendors and other service providers that empower our clients to grow, both domestically and internationally.

OUR VISION

  • To deliver an uncompromising level of advice & work quality
  • To be committed to our client’s business and success
  • To have an in-house approach and mindset
  • To earn the trust of our client partners
  • To be highly available and responsive
